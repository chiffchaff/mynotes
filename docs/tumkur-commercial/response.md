## 5. Budget-Focused Strategies

### ₹25L–₹50L Strategy

**Best Options:**
- **600-800 sq ft commercial plot** on BH Road/SIT Road periphery
- **Highway frontage land** (30x40 or 40x60) near Ring Road/Kyathsandra
- **Small retail shops** (200-300 sq ft) in established layouts

**Expected Returns:**
- Rental yield: **6.5-8%**
- 5-year appreciation: **60-90%**
- Rental start: **6-18 months** (plots) / **immediate** (pre-leased shops)

**Risks:** Lower liquidity, construction dependency, tenant availability gaps

---

### ₹50L–₹1Cr Strategy

**Best Options:**
- **Corner commercial plots** (1,200-2,000 sq ft) on BH Road/SIT Road
- **Pre-leased retail units** (400-800 sq ft ground floor)
- **Mixed-use land** with immediate development potential

**Expected Returns:**
- Monthly rental: **₹35,000–₹75,000**
- Rental yield: **6-7.5%**
- 5-year appreciation: **75-120%**
- Pre-leased viability: **Good** (7-9% cap rates available)

**Exit Options:** REIT-ready assets, institutional buyers, or portfolio sale

## 10. Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|---------------------|
| **Liquidity** | Focus on BH/SIT Road; avoid peripheral zones |
| **Tenant availability** | Pre-lease agreements; avoid oversupply pockets |
| **Agricultural-to-commercial conversion** | Verify DC conversion; avoid greenfield farmland |
| **Layout approvals** | Purchase only in approved/layout-sanctioned areas |
| **Oversupply** | Avoid emerging corridors with heavy upcoming supply |
